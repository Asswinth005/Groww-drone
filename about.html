OUR STORY

From a rooftop to the skies

It started with a conversation on a rooftop in downtown LA. Our founder, James, was a commercial photographer who borrowed a drone from a friend to capture the sunset. What he saw from above changed everything—the city lights, the hidden patterns, the stories that only reveal themselves from altitude.

That evening, he realized that the most powerful perspectives are the ones we rarely get to see. He sold his studio lights, bought his first drone, and never looked down again.

"In 2019, SKY VUW was born with a simple idea: help people see their world—homes, events, landmarks—from a perspective that inspires awe."

James Carter

founder & lead pilot

What We Believe

Our Core Values

These principles guide every flight, every edit, and every interaction with our clients and communities.

Safety First

Every flight is planned with FAA standards, dual checks, and real‑time monitoring. No shortcuts, ever.

Creative Excellence

We don't do templates. Each project gets a custom storyboard, cinematic grade, and artistic vision.

Environmental Respect

Low noise, minimal disruption, and full consideration for wildlife and natural habitats.

Integrity & Transparency

Clear communication, honest pricing, and no hidden fees. What we promise is what we deliver.

Community Connection

We work with local organizations, obtain necessary permits, and respect the spaces we fly over.

Continuous Innovation

We invest in the latest technology and training to bring fresh perspectives and cutting-edge techniques.
