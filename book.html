Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking an aerial shoot

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for real estate and events, and 4-6 weeks for weddings to ensure availability. However, we do accommodate last-minute requests when possible – just give us a call.
What happens if weather is bad on the day?
Safety is our priority. If conditions are unsafe (high winds, rain, low clouds), we'll reschedule at no cost. For time-sensitive shoots, we monitor forecasts closely and communicate early.
Do I need to obtain permits for drone flights?
We handle all FAA authorizations and local permits. Just provide the location, and we'll ensure everything is legal and safe. Our team is experienced in navigating restricted airspace.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations made 48+ hours before the shoot receive a full refund. Within 48 hours, the 20% deposit is non-refundable. We understand emergencies – just contact us as soon as possible.
Do you travel outside Los Angeles?
Absolutely! We serve clients worldwide. Travel fees may apply depending on distance and duration – we'll provide a custom quote during booking.
